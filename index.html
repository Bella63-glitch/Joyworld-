Empowering Lives Through Knowledge

"My name is Hannah Njuguna, an education enthusiast, visionary leader, and the proud Founder and CEO of Joyworld Training Institute..."

Established in December 2023, my journey is rooted deeply in the field of education—a path that has shaped my purpose, values, and commitment to transforming lives through knowledge.

From an early stage, I developed a strong passion for learning. Over time, I recognized a gap between traditional education systems and the practical skills needed in today’s fast-changing world. This realization became the foundation of Joyworld.

I envisioned a training center that not only educates but also inspires confidence, independence, and growth. My mission is to ensure Joyworld becomes a center of transformation where individuals from all walks of life can unlock their potential.

The journey has just begun, and I remain committed to building a legacy that empowers communities and shapes a brighter future for generations to come.

Hannah Njuguna

Founder & CEO, Joyworld
